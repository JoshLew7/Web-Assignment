About Me

Who I Am?

Hey, my name is Joshua Lewis. I am 19 years old and was born and raised in a small town called Saint Rose, Louisiana. I completed the majority of my schooling in Louisiana, but in my senior year of high school, I moved to Heath, Texas. I think this move fits my personality as I love meeting new people and making new memories!

What I Do?

I am currently a rising sophomore Computer Science major at Xavier University of Louisiana and am expected to graduate in May of 2023! I am a software mentor for a local FRC (Robotics) Team in my community as well as a tutor for some of my peers at Xavier. Also I am currently working at Google this summer as a STEP intern which is one of the things I am most proud of!

Why I do it?

I got into computer science because of Robotics during my freshman year of high-school and from that point on I've been obsessed with it. Seeing the code that I write affect a robot was something that never got old. My goal as I get further into my career is to work in the robotics health industry or the autonomous vehicle industry. This is because those are areas where a lot of people can be positively affected as well as an area that I truly enjoy working in. My ultimate dream is to one day own my own company!

Here are some of my projects!

Overnightly

Developed Overnightly, an app that eases travel planning by algorithmically finding a place to stay based on given locations, suggesting places based on filters, and planning optimal itinerary routes.

FIRST Robotics

FIRST Robotics Competition is a global challenge that brings together over 8000 teams from across the world to compete in a six week STEM based challenge. Every year a new game is released and you compete both locally and globally to be considered World Champions.

PokeHood

PokeHood is a program that I designed along with two other partners for my Google CSSI final project. Designed in Processing Python, PokeHood has most of the basic features that would be present in an old pokemon game. For the project, I designed all of the in-game mechanics such as the world with character movements/animation and player to pokemon detection to initiate the battles. I also designed the battle screen logic such as type effectiveness and damage calculation.

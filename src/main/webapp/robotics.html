FIRST ROBOTICS

FIRST Robotics Competition is a high school robotics challenge that brings together students from all across the world to compete in new challenges every-year. At the start of the season in January, teams watch the game revel that will tell them the challenge that they have to design for that year. After the game reveal, teams have six weeks to create a robot that can preform those task to the best of it's ability. Then they will go into the competition phase where they will compete locally and globally in order to be crowned Einstein Champions.
